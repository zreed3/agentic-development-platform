# ADG Surface Map

Use this to make visible and hidden surfaces explicit.

## Capture

- feature id, route or command, file anchor, and lifecycle status;
- entity, workflow, and action set;
- persona and role states for allowed, read-only, forbidden, locked, hidden, future, partial, and deprecated surfaces;
- empty, error, loading, validation, recovery, and destructive-action states;
- route, browser, or contract test evidence.

## Rules

- Navigation truth, direct URL truth, and contract truth must agree.
- Do not expose unfinished work as live.
- Record unknown states as gaps instead of inventing behavior.
